01 — Profile

Research depth. Engineering instincts.

I specialize in deep learning for computer vision, particularly when data is limited, domains shift, or solutions need to run outside a notebook. My work combines self-supervised learning, Vision Transformers, and image analysis with practical software delivery.

At DIPC and UPV/EHU, I collaborate closely with domain experts to make advanced imaging models useful to the people making scientific decisions with them.
