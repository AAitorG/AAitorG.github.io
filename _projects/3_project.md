An **NLP** project exploring transformer-based text classification.

## What I built

- Built an end-to-end pipeline that fine-tunes **BERT** for automated classification of
  news as reliable or fake.
- Handled the full workflow: data preprocessing and tokenization, model fine-tuning, and
  evaluation.

## Why it matters

It applies the same **Transformer** foundations I use in computer vision to the language
domain, reinforcing modality-agnostic deep-learning fundamentals.
