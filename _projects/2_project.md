A self-directed project to deepen my understanding of **reinforcement learning** and
**evolutionary algorithms** — topics outside my immediate PhD scope.

## What I built

- Implemented the **NEAT (NeuroEvolution of Augmenting Topologies)** algorithm from
  scratch in Python.
- Designed the game environment, the fitness functions, and the network-evolution logic.
- Optimized the implementation for performance to allow faster generations and more
  efficient search of the topology space.

## Result

The bot consistently reaches the **2048 tile and beyond**, a testament to implementing
complex algorithms from first principles.
